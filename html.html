BOX MODEL

A box model is the design and layout of your page which wraps around every HTML element and it consists of the Margin, Border, Padding and the Content

  • Margin- It is the transparent area around the border.
  • Border- It is the area around the padding and content.
  • Padding- It is the transparent area that provides spacing around the content.
  • Content- It is the area which contain the text and images.
